About Me

My name is Robert Ruttenberg. This is a simple website that explains a little bit about me while demonstrating a grasp of the basics of HTML and CSS.

Please note that what this simple webpage does not do is reflect my sound aesthetic judgement. When first learning a skill, I embrace the opportunity to be daringly and flamboyantly awful at it, and so that is what I have done here. I will save good taste for after I have learned the basics.

I am interested in exploring web development because I have had several ideas over the last couple years for various web apps, but despite some programming experience as an undergraduate, I have felt I lacked the skills to pursue them in any meaningful way. Yes, in theory I could partner with someone who did have the proper skills--perhaps a more economical option than teaching myself--but I have a strong and stubborn DIY streak. I do not like to throw things out and I do not like to buy things; I do like to fix things and I do like to build things.
